Understanding Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)

Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) is a government initiative that allows financial subsidies and benefits to be directly transferred to the bank accounts of eligible citizens. It aims to reduce leakage, improve transparency, and ensure timely delivery of benefits. By digitizing the process, DBT empowers individuals, promotes financial inclusion, and simplifies access to scholarships, pensions, and other welfare schemes.

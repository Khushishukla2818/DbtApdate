Know the Difference

Understanding key concepts for successful scholarship application

What is Aadhaar Linking?

Aadhaar linking is the process of connecting your Aadhaar number with your bank account or other services.

Examples:

  • Linking Aadhaar with bank account for subsidies
  • Connecting Aadhaar with mobile number
  • Linking Aadhaar with PAN card
Learn More

What does DBT Enabled Mean?

DBT Enabled means your bank account can receive direct government transfers without delays.

Benefits:

  • Direct transfer without intermediaries
  • Faster processing of scholarships
  • Reduced paperwork and delays
Learn More

What is Aadhaar Seeding?

Aadhaar seeding is specifically linking your Aadhaar with your bank account for DBT purposes.

Process Steps:

  • Visit your bank or use online portal
  • Provide Aadhaar number and bank details
  • Verify and confirm seeding
